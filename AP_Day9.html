Overview:

This module gives an overview of shifts in instruction as seen through the Common Core Standards as a model of College and Career Readiness Standards. Participants also develop a rationale for how the Math- standards are used to drive high quality instruction.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Describe each of the CCSS Math shifts and explain how the shifts frame and impact rigorous instructional non-negotiables in both the elementary and secondary grades (2.1a)
  2. Explain how the Math standards frame instructional planning, providing for the development of learning activities and classroom implementation strategies (2.1a)
