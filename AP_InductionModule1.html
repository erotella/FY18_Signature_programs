Session Overview:

This module introduces participants to the “New Leaders Way” of cohort engagement. Participants will spend time building a strong sense of community through various community building activities that will center the identity of the cohort as a part of the New Leaders family of exceptional leaders. They will also be introduced to New Leaders Vision, Mission, and Core Belief and the staff members who live by and support the New Leaders Way. During this module, participants begin to develop their capacity to articulate the vision, mission and core beliefs of the organization and explore the Transformational Leadership Framework that operationalizes the collective work of New Leaders.

Session Outcomes:

By the end of the session, Residents will:

  • Engage in community building activities to enhance cohort relationships.
  • Develop a deeper learning about themselves and others as they explore strengths, learning opportunities, and blind spots.
  • Interpret and make connections to the New Leaders mission, vision, and core values.
  • Get to know New Leaders staff.
  • Develop and practice individual articulations of the “why” that includes motivation for joining New Leaders and make personal connections to the vision/mission/core value statement.
  • Identify actions that exemplify belief-based, goal-driven leadership.
