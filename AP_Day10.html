Overview:

As part II of the Foundations of Standards Based Instructional Leadership-Math, participants develop their capacity to observe lessons, look for key evidence of core instruction, and develop an initial critique to prepare for post observation meetings.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Identify what strong standards-aligned Math instruction looks like in the elementary and secondary grades (2.1a)
  2. Observe instruction and identify teacher and student core actions aligned to Common Core instructional practices (2.3a)
  3. Utilize the Coaching - Instructional Practice Guide tool to identify patterns and/or gaps in instructional practice as a means to ensure development, implementation, and evaluation of consistency in routines and instructional strategies throughout a school (2.1b)
