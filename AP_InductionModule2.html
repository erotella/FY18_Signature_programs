Session Overview:

This module introduces participants to 2 primary tools that will be throughout the course of Summer Foundations. Participants have opportunity to engage with the first tool, the School Diagnostic through analysis activities. The 2nd tool, the Aspiring Principals Waterfront School Case Study, anchors the second half of this module, giving participants time to explore the content and contexts captured within the case study.

Session Outcomes:

By the end of the session, Residents will:

  • Engaged in community building activities to enhance cohort relationships.
  • Previewed the School Diagnostic & used it to determine relevance and quality of evidence collected to diagnose current state of school effectiveness.
  • Surveyed the Waterfront Case Study for evidence collection to use with the Transformational Leadership Framework as a means of anchoring use competence for Summer Foundations learning.
