Overview:

This module introduces participants to the important work of doing school diagnosis using the New Leaders school diagnostic tool. Participants spend time designing SMART goals as a result of the findings from the New Leaders school diagnostic tool. These goals are then applied to strategic planning principles as a process of leading in a school effectively.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Build capacity to identify root cause issues in a school in order to prioritize with goal setting (5.1a)
  2. Understand how designing SMART goals ensure equity and academic success for all students aligned to vision and mission. (3.1a)
  3. Create key benchmarks and action steps as a part of strategic planning with key stakeholders (5.1c)
